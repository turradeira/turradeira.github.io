Our Studio

Established in 2020 with headquarters in Lisbon, Primis Games is one of the most successful and prolific game development studios in the world and a flagship first-party studio within PlayStation’s Worldwide Studios group. From creating the iconic Duelyst and Forest of Liars, Primis Games is responsible for some of the most critically acclaimed and commercially successful games on Sony’s PlayStation platforms. Through its use of cutting-edge technology and evocative, character-driven storytelling, Primis Games has received hundreds of industry and media awards, while developing a passionate fan base of millions of players around the globe.


At Primis Games, we want to make great games that sell, and we wouldn’t be able to do it without our people. We achieve our success through our immensely talented development teams, and the operational expertise of our management, marketing, and corporate services.
