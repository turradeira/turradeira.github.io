Support

  • If the game is not running, please try to close the game and start it again first. If that doesn't help, try to uninstall and install it. If that still proves to be ineffective, please contact us through the SUPPORT contact form below. Also feel free to just share your opinion or leave a suggestion!

  • If you want to report any technical issues (glitches, bugs etc): please be specific about the game, platform and device/hardware (Nexus 10, iPad mini 4, Windows PC etc.) and your system (e.g. iOS 9.3, Windows 10 etc.).

  • Regarding Linux support in our games, we use various technologies and try to support Linux where it's possible, but unfortunately sometimes it's hard or impossible to provide stable Linux versions.
